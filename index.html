About Mwanje Frank Abdallah

Mwanje Frank Abdallah is a proud Muslim and a dedicated youth leader from Gomba, Uganda. As the founder of the Youth Capacity Building Network (YCBN), he is committed to empowering the next generation through mentorship and community development. A proud alumnus of Makerere University with a Bachelor of Social Work, his life's work is driven by a passion for social change and a belief in the power of faith.

My Journey & Impact

Former MUSAU President

As the former President of the Makerere University Muslim Students' Association (MUSAU), Mwanje championed student welfare and promoted interfaith dialogue.

Promoting Islam

He has dedicated his efforts to promoting and strengthening the practice of Islam in schools and local communities, fostering a deeper understanding of the faith.

Charity & Empowerment

Through the Youth Capacity Building Network and other projects, Mwanje has spearheaded numerous charity initiatives and youth empowerment programs.
