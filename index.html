About Mwanje Frank Abdallah

A concise biography

Biography

Mwanje Frank Abdallah is a proud Muslim and an ardent lover of youth development. Born and raised in Gomba, Uganda, Frank pursued a Bachelor of Social Work at Makerere University where he developed his passion for community service and leadership. As former President of the Makerere University Muslim Students Association (MUSAU), he led student outreach programs, charity initiatives and inter-school faith activities that strengthened community ties and supported vulnerable students.

Today, as Founder and Executive Director of Youth Capacity Building Network (YCBN), Frank mentors thousands of young people across Uganda — equipping them with leadership skills, vocational guidance, and moral support centered on faith and service.
